## Why WildCity?

- **Real-world city-scale multimodal dataset.**
- **6 U.S. cities, 1,000+ km of roads, 2.14M keyframes.**
- **Benchmark** for scalable urban reconstruction and extrapolated rendering.
- **Testbed** for closed-loop simulation and spatial intelligence.

</HighlightedSection>

{/* §4 — Dataset */}

## Dataset

WildCity is collected from real-world urban driving across six U.S. cities, with synchronized surround-view cameras, LiDAR, IMU, and GPS.

| Category | WildCity |
| :--- | :--- |
| Cities | 6 U.S. cities |
| Road coverage | 1,000+ km |
| Trajectories | 18 |
| Keyframes | 2.14M |
| Cameras | 6 surround-view RGB |
| RGB resolution / FPS | 1440×928 / 10 Hz |
| LiDAR | 10 Hz |
| IMU / GPS | 10 Hz / 2 Hz |

{/* §7 — Release Scope & Privacy */}

## Release Scope & Privacy

- We release **anonymized** sensor data only.
- Faces and license plates are **blurred** (e.g., with EgoBlur).
- Raw, **unblurred** images are **not** distributed.
- Released modalities: **RGB, LiDAR, calibration, poses, masks, train/val/test splits, code, and configs**.
- Sensitive locations are handled with care and excluded where appropriate.
- Distributed under a documented **license / terms of use**.
- Access is granted via a **dataset request form** (link coming soon).
